WAYS TO DONATE

WISER relies on your gifts to fund our vision of a world where girls are healthy,safe,educated,and leading their communities.Check out the many ways to give below,or Contact us to discuss your giving.

ONLINE DONATIONS

The quickest,easiest way to show your support is to donate online now! Make a one-time gift,or setup recurring gifts for continued impact.

CORPORATE MATCH

Ask your employer about corporate philanthropy programs to match your donations to WISER! If you have questions or would like to start a matching program at your company, please contact our Development Manager, Emily Dake at emily.dake@wisergirls.org or (919) 307-6396.

PLANNED GIVING

Consider cementing your legacy as a WISER champion through bequests and other planned gifts to support our work for generations to come. To discuss planned giving, please contact our Development Manager, Emily Dake at emily.dake@wisergirls.org or (919) 307-6396.

IN KIND

WISER works with partners who provide goods and services like menstrual supplies, STEM equipment, and professional services like tech support, legal consulting, and more. Contact us to learn more.

AMAZON SMILE

Support WISER with 0.5% of each eligible purchase you make on Amazon! Visit smile.amazon.com, click on the AmazonSmile dropdown menu, search for WISER Girls International, and select us as your charity!
