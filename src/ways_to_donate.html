How to Make Your WISER Gift

WAYS TO DONATE

WISER relies on your gifts to fund our vision of a world where girls are healthy,safe,educated,and leading their communities. Check out the many ways to give below,or Contact us to discuss your giving.

ONLINE DONATIONS

The quickest, easiest way to show your support is to donate online now! Make a one-time gift, or setup recurring gifts for continued impact.

PAYPAL

AMAZON SMILE

Support WISER with 0.5% of each eligible purchase you make on Amazon! Visit smile.amazon.com, click on the AmazonSmile dropdown menu, search for WISER Girls International, and select us as your charity!

IN KIND

WISER works with partners who provide goods and services like menstrual supplies, STEM equipment, and professional services like tech support, legal consulting, and more. Contact us at info@wisergirls.org to learn more.

PLANNED GIVING

Consider cementing your legacy as a WISER champion through bequests and other planned gifts to support our work for generations to come. To discuss planned giving, please contact us at info@wisergirls.org

MPESA

WISER accepts Kenyan Shilling (KES) donations via M-Pesa through a partnership with Global Giving and M-Changa. Click here to learn more!

WIRE AND STOCK TRANSFERS

To donate by wire or stock transfer, please contact our Director of Finance and Operations, Michelle Gherardi at michelle.gherardi@wisergirls.org

Ask your employer about corporate philanthropy programs to match your donations to WISER! If you have questions or would like to start a matching program at your company, please contact us at info@wisergirls.org
